While I'm not learning and advancing my knowledge in code to better my techniques and skills, I do some of the following to maintain a healthy work-life balance.

Bouldering

Bouldering is rock climbing stripped down to its raw essentials. Leaving behind ropes and harnesses and just using climbing shoes and a bag of chalk over safety mats, your challenge is to climb short but tricky bouldering "problems" (a route, or sequence of moves) using balance, technique, strength, and your brain.

I've been bouldering on and off for the past 8 months and quite enjoy the challenge that comes with analyzing and problem solving the route, and where to place your hands and feet for which rock to reach towards the top.
